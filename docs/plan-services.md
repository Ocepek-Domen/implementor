### 6. How we work — extended (S1)
A more detailed version of the homepage process section. Same 4 steps, but each step expands with a paragraph and concrete deliverables.

**Step 1 — Discover (free, 45 min)**
- Process mapping conversation
- Honest fit assessment
- Recommended Odoo modules
- Rough timeline and budget range
- Deliverable: a one-page recommendation email after the call

**Step 2 — Design (paid, 2–4 weeks for mid-market)**
- Detailed workshop with your team
- Process documentation
- Module list with configurations
- Integration architecture diagram
- Data migration plan
- Fixed-price implementation proposal
- Deliverable: a written Statement of Work you own

**Step 3 — Build (4 weeks to several months, depending on scope)**
- Sprint-based delivery with weekly demos
- Configuration, customization, integration, data migration
- User acceptance testing with your team
- Training sessions for admins and end-users
- Deliverable: a working Odoo instance with your data, your users trained

**Step 4 — Live & grow (ongoing)**
- 30-day hypercare window post go-live
- Quarterly health checks
- New feature additions as your business changes
- SLA-backed support (response times defined)
